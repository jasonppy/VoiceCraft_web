VoiceCraft: Zero-Shot Speech Editing and Text-to-Speech in the Wild

Puyuan Peng1, Po-Yao Huang2, Daniel Li, Abdelrahman Mohamed3, David Harwath1,
1The University of Texas at Austin 2FAIR 3Rembrand
Paper Code&Model

TL;DR

VoiceCraft is a token infilling neural codec language model, that achieves state-of-the-art performance on both speech editing and zero-shot text-to-speech (TTS) on in-the-wild data including audiobooks, internet videos, and podcasts.

To clone an unseen voice or edit a recording, VoiceCraft needs only a few seconds of the voice.
